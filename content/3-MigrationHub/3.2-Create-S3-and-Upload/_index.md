## Create S3 Bucket & Upload (Images 5–10)

1) Open **Amazon S3**.  
![5](/images/erp/5.png)

2) Click **Create bucket**.  
![6](/images/erp/6.png)

3) Enter bucket name.  
![7](/images/erp/7.png)

4) Review and create.  
![8](/images/erp/8.png)

5) Bucket created.  
![9](/images/erp/9.png)

6) Open the bucket → **Upload** the inventory file.  
![10](/images/erp/10.png)
